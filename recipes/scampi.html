How to Make: Scrimp Scampi

Shrimp scampi is an italian dish that makes the mouth salivate when done making. It is a fan favorite in american culture for simpleness and taste effective for a cheap and effective price.

Ingredients
  • Butter - 4 tablespoons
  • Olive Oil - 1 tablespoon
  • Garlic(mince) - 3 cloves
  • Red Pepper Flakes(crushed or to taste)
  • Shrimp(peeled and deveined - 1 pound
  • Dry White Wine - 1/2 cup
  • Fresh Parsley(chopped) - 1/2 cup
  • Kosher Salt and Pepper - add to taste
Steps
  1. Add the butter and olive oil to a large skillet over medium heat
  2. Stir in the garlic and red pepper flakes. Cook for 1 minute
  3. Add the shrimp to the pan and toss to coat. Cook the shrimp until bright pink and no longer translucent, approximately 2-3 minutes.
  4. Using a slotted spoon, transfer the shrimp to a plate and keep warm.
  5. Add teh wine to the pan and cook for 2-3 minutes. Turn off the heat.
  6. Return the shrimp to the pan and toss to coat in the sauce. Sprinkle with the parsley. Season with salt and pepper, to taste. Serve immediately.
